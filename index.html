How it works

In order to understand how our device works, we first need to explain the difference between orgasm and ejaculation: they’re not actually the same thing. An orgasm is the pleasure you feel at the peak of sex, it occurs first. Soon after, comes the ejaculation (pun intended), which is essentially the release of semen. When a man ejaculates, various chemicals like oxytocin, among others, are released in the brain ending the orgasm and preventing others for some time. If a man delays or prevents (no cleanup) his ejaculation, he can achieve multiple orgasms.

So how do you delay ejaculation? It’s possible using the Pelvic Floor Muscle (PFM), frequently referred to as kegel muscles. This is the same muscle that allows you to stop and start urination. By training this muscle and flexing it at the moment of ejaculation, a man can prevent it and have multiple orgasms that are longer and stronger. It can be quite difficult to train and consciously control this while having sex.

Our device is a minimally intrusive “anal plug” that will use electronic muscle stimulating technology to train and flex your PFM with ease. You insert the device and wear a remote control ring on your finger. When you are about to ejaculate, you push the button, and your PFM is automatically flexed to prevent ejaculation. This ultimately allows you to keep going, as long as you keep pressing the button at the right time. It may take 2-4 weeks of use before the device works well since you will need to strengthen your PFM initially.
